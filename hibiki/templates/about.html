About Hibiki

Our Mission

Hibiki is dedicated to bringing music lovers closer to their favorite artists. We aim to provide a seamless streaming experience with high-quality sound, curated playlists, and personalized recommendations.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Hibiki started as a small project by a team of passionate music enthusiasts. Over time, we have grown into a full-fledged music platform that supports both listeners and artists alike.

Our Values

  • High-quality music streaming experience
  • User-first design and interface
  • Supporting independent artists
  • Privacy and security for our users
  • Innovation in music discovery
